# Sample UI Sketches for Demo

## How to Create Demo Sketches

Use a black marker on white paper. Draw bold, clear shapes. Label elements with text if desired.

## DRAWING TIPS

1. **Use thick lines** - Thin lines may not detect well
2. **Clear spacing** - Leave gaps between elements
3. **Label elements** - Write "Button", "Email", etc. inside shapes
4. **Good lighting** - Avoid shadows when photographing
5. **White background** - Plain white paper works best
6. **Portrait orientation** - Match phone screen

---

## PHOTO TIPS

1. **Flat angle** - Take photo straight down
2. **Good lighting** - Natural light or bright room
3. **No shadows** - Your hand/phone shadow can interfere
4. **Fill frame** - Sketch should fill most of the camera view
5. **Steady hand** - Avoid blur
